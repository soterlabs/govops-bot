## Summary

| Coverage | Count | With notif. time | Without |
|----------|-------|-------------------|---------|
| All 3 sources | 7 | 7 | 0 |
| 2 sources | 8 | 4 | 4 |
| Table only | 8 | 8 | 0 |

| **Total** | **51** | **20** | **31** |

**20 of 51 events have a notification time** — all 19 from the Coordination
Table plus 1 from the Atlas (#45 Proposals posted to Forum, Fri 08:00 UTC).

The remaining **31 events have no specific time**. All 20 Diagram-only events
and 7 of 8 Atlas-only events lack a time. Among the 2-source events, 4 have
no time (#9, #10, #14, #15) — these come from the Diagram + Atlas pair, which
never specifies precise deadlines.

The 7 events confirmed by all 3 sources form the backbone of the spell cycle.
The Diagram is the most detailed source (27 events total), but many of its
items are operational artifacts (Immunefi, Bug Bounty, GovAlpha Review) that
neither the Table nor the Atlas mention — it is unclear whether these should
trigger bot notifications. The Table has the most precise timing (specific
UTC deadlines) but omits the entire W1 Monday phase and several W2 Thursday
artifacts. The Atlas is the most authoritative but least granular — it
describes the process flow without day-by-day deadlines.
